Settlement Risk Monitor

Prediction-market prices are only as reliable as the contracts behind them. This site tracks disputed contracts, settlement chains, rule clarity, and historical revision rates — so traders, journalists, and platforms can see when a price carries settlement risk, not just event risk.

Analyze a market Search by title, URL, slug, or condition ID. Cite check See whether a price can be cited as a clean event probability.
