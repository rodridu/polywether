About this monitor

Hongzhen Du, Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University.

The Settlement Risk Monitor is the companion site to “How Prediction Markets Resolve: Evidence from Polymarket.” It makes the paper’s resolved- and disputed-contract panels browsable for traders, journalists, and researchers.

Methodology, sample construction, limitations, and replication materials are on the Research & Data page. Data schema and JSON endpoints are in the Docs. Source: github.com/rodridu/polywether.

Acknowledgment

The site scaffolding (page structure, CSS conventions, static-data / JSON-endpoint pattern, vanilla-JS rendering) is adapted from the Bellwether project’s open-source platform code, built by Andrew B. Hall (Stanford GSB / Hoover Institution) and Elliot Paschal. Bellwether tracks price robustness in prediction markets; this site is a separate artifact tracking settlement robustness for disputed Polymarket-UMA contracts.

Substantive content (paper, data pipeline, settlement-path taxonomy, text-feature regex, settlement-risk tier rule) is independent work. Errors in the adaptation are mine. This repo is not formally affiliated with Bellwether or the Hoover Institution.
