Where settlement risk concentrates

The headline 24.1% revision rate is for the disputed sample as a whole. The risk map breaks it down: by category, by chain type, by whether the contract names a source, and by whether it specifies a fallback. All rates are conditional on D=1 (disputed).

Source: data/conditional_rates.json, derived from the same 2,221-disputed-contract panel feeding the Explorer. Rates are sample shares, not estimates with uncertainty intervals — small-N buckets should be read with that in mind.
