The linter checks whether the rule text contains the fields that usually make settlement auditable: named source, fallback, edge cases, time zone, settlement timing, and invalid/N/A treatment.

    What this analyzer does NOT do
    • It does not measure liquidity or price robustness. For that, use Bellwether.
    • It does not predict settlement. The flags identify whether the relevant language appears in the rule text — not whether the contract is correctly designed.
    • It does not look up settlement chains. If the market is in the disputed-contract panel, use Analyze for the full record.
    • The flags are heuristic regex over a noisy text corpus. False positives and false negatives both occur.
