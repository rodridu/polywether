Chain View

How a single contract gets to its final payoff

Each disputed contract goes through one or more requests. A request can settle cleanly, be disputed, or be voided by UMA's Data Verification Mechanism (DVM), forcing a re-request. The examples below trace this path for ten hand-curated sports contracts, split between the two main chain types. Click "Open contract detail" on any card for the full record.

Selection rule: contracts in the disputed sample (D=1) with at least two episodes, sports category only (low public-controversy risk), proposed and final outcomes both observed. Hand-curated to span both chain types.
