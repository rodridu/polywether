Why are some rows showing [question text missing]? Question-text coverage is 79.1% (1,756 / 2,221).

465 of 2,221 disputed contracts (20.9%) have no recoverable Polymarket-side metadata — question, resolution description, AND UMA ancillary text all null. The settlement chain (UMA RequestPrice / DisputePrice / SettlePrice) is still observed: chain type, first proposal, final payoff, multi-episode flag, and the audit checklist all populate normally for these rows. They are tagged settlement_risk_tier = High via the Rule text not recoverable rule.

Three known causes (in priority): off-Polymarket UMA requests that never indexed back to a market record, condition_id schema drift between rev3 panel and pm_markets, and metadata purged before the 2026-04-23 snapshot. See Research → Limitations for the full coverage discussion. Filter to these rows with the Question text: Missing chip below.
